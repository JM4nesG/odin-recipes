Baked Oatmeal1

Description

This baked oatmeal recipe hails from Pennsylvania Amish country. Everyone who tries it loves it! Serve this comforting dish topped with milk.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons bakingpowder
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 melted butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup dried cranberries

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients. Preheat the over to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Mix Together oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt in s lsrge browl.
  3. Beat in eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Stir in dried cranberries.
  4. Spread into a 9x13-inch banking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated over until top is golden, about 40 minutes. Serve and enjoy!
